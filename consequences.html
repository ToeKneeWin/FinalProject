What are the consequences of Virtual Reality?

Besides the possibility of running into walls and furniture there are other consequences of VR

  • Tracking of video, audio, movement, size, and other data
  • motion sickness
  • Seizures
  • Eye soreness
  • Addiction
  • Desensitized
  • Anti-Socializing
